Refund Policy

Our refund policy is as follows:

Annual Membership:
If you cancel within the first 90 days of your membership, you will not receive a refund, and your membership will end 90 days from your start date.

If you cancel within the first 180 days but after the first 90 days, you will receive a $100 refund, and your membership will end 180 days from your start date.

If you cancel after the first 180 days, you will not receive a refund, and your membership will end 365 days from your start date.

6-Month Membership:
If you cancel your 6-month membership, you will not receive a refund, and your membership will end 180 days from your start date.

Monthly Membership:
If you cancel your monthly membership, you will not receive a refund, and your membership will end 30 days from your start date.

*Please note that all refunds are subject to the terms and conditions outlined in our membership agreement.
