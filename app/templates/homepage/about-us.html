Refund Policy

Our refund policy is as follows:

For Annual Memberships:

- If you cancel within 90 days of the start date, there will be no refund, and the membership will be valid for 90 days from the start date.

- If you cancel after 90 days but within 180 days of the start date, a refund of $100 will be issued, and the membership will be valid for 180 days from the start date.

- If you cancel after 180 days of the start date, there will be no refund, and the membership will be valid for one year from the start date.

For Other Membership Types:

- Refunds are not available for Monthly and 6 Months membership types.

*Please note that all refunds are subject to the terms and conditions outlined in our membership agreement.
