Welcome to Countryside Community Swimming Club

Our club is a community swimming club that offers a range of swimming lessons for all ages and abilities. We are a friendly club and welcome all new members. Our club is based in the heart of the countryside and we have a range of facilities including a 25m indoor pool, a sauna and a steam room. We also have a fully equipped gym and a cafe. Our club is open 7 days a week and we have a range of membership options available. We also offer a range of social events and activities for our members. We look forward to welcoming you to our club.
