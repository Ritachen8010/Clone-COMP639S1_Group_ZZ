Our Club

Welcome to our swimming club, where we offer a variety of activities for all ages and abilities. Our range of services includes one-on-one swimming lessons and dynamic group aerobics classes, ensuring there's something for everyone.

Our current aerobics classes offering: Zumba, Aqua Fit, low-impact sessions, and special mums and babies classes, among others. Each class is designed to offer a unique and enjoyable way to stay fit and healthy.

Our mission is to providing a safe and welcoming environment for everyone to learn to swim and have fun. We invite you to join our community and experience the joy of swimming.

We are excited to announce that our club is now accepting new bookings. Our schdule are open from 6 AM to 8 PM, seven days a week, throughout the year, with the exception of Christmas Day and Boxing Day.

Our membership is open to all, and we offer a range of packages to suit your needs. Annually: 700$, Monthly: 60$.

Don't miss out on the chance to be part of our swimming family. Come and dive into the fun!
